The Genesis

Selo, coined from self-love, is a company created to help people find love for themselves. It all began when I realised that as much as I encouraged others to love themselves, I didn't really love myself. My feelings of discomfort stemmed from not having a sense of ownership in my environment, lack of confidence in my appearance and major discomfort in public spaces. In light of this, I decided to embark on a journey to find self-love and to help others do the same.

Mission

Selo's mission is to reach people in every corner of the world and help them achieve self-love.

Vision

In Selo's vision, people are able to cultivate self-love by creating a sense of pride and satisfaction in taking care of oneself through affordable, high-quality products.

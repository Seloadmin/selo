The Menstrual Kit (Standard Kit)

This kit is equipped with everything you could possibly need during this sensitive period of time.

Contents include: Sanitary towels(pads), hand sanitizer, body mist, tissue, wipes, soap, disposable nylons, disposable pant, portable bidet, the monthlies diary, and an exclusive pen.

Functions of some of the items include:
Portable bidet: It provides a stream of water for personal cleansing instead of bathroom tissue.
The monthlies diary: Helps you to record your period for medical use or personal monitoring.
Exclusive pen: This pen can serve as a phone stand and also act as a stylus pen for your phone.

The Menstrual Kit (Standard Renewal Kit)

This is a follow up kit that provides you with the items that need to be renewed every month.

Contents include: Sanitary towels(pads), hand sanitizer, body mist, tissue, wipes, soap, disposable nylons, and disposable pant.

Note: This kit excludes the bidet, exclusive pen and the monthlies diary. These items can only be gained when you buy the standard kit

The Monthlies Diary

This is a journal in which a woman writes down information about her menstrual cycle, such as the date of her last period, the length of her cycle, and any symptoms she is experiencing.
